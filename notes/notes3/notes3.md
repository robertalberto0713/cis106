## Commands Definitions

## clear
* **Definition**:
  * clears the screen
* **Usage**:
  * `clear`
* **Example**:
  * How to clear the screen: 
    * `clear`

## echo
* **Definition:**
  * Displays text on the screen
* **Usage:**
  * `echo` + `option` + `string to display`
* **Example:**
  * How to display a line of text:
    * `echo "hello"`
  * How to display 2 lines of text
    * `echo -e "hello\nworld"`
